The following expressions are proposed for the magnitude of its acceleration a:

I.    a=2s(t2-t1)2

II.   a=v22-v122s

III.  a=v2-v1t2-t1

Which is correct?


A.  I and II only

B.  I and III only

C.  II and III only

D.  I, II and III
