A flash of light sent by an Earth observer at ct = 2.0 km is directed towards the spaceship.

Estimate, using the spacetime diagram, the time in seconds when the flash of light reaches the spaceship according to the Earth observer.

[2]

Markscheme

Line drawn at 45° from ct = 2 km to hit spaceship world line at ct = 5 km

OR

ct = 1.2/(c − 0.6c) + 2 = 5 «km» ✓

t = 5000c = 1.7 × 10−5 «s» ✓
