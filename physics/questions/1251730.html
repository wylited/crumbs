A projectile is launched with a velocity u at an angle θ to the horizontal. It reaches a maximum height s. What is the time taken to reach the maximum height?


A.  2su cos θ

B.  2sg

C.  u cos θg

D.  u sin θg

[1]

Markscheme

D

Examiners report

This question is very easily answered by elimination. The time to reach max height is the same as for a projectile launched vertically with initial speed u sin θ, and no other alternative than D contains the term u sin θ.
