A body is held in translational equilibrium by three coplanar forces of magnitude 3 N, 4 N and 5 N. Three statements about these forces are

I.  all forces are perpendicular to each other
II.  the forces cannot act in the same direction
III.  the vector sum of the forces is equal to zero.

Which statements are true?

A.  I and II only

B.  I and III only

C.  II and III only

D.  I, II and III

[1]

Markscheme

C
