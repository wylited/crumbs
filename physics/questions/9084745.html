(ii)

No unbalanced external forces act on the system of the curling stones. Outline why the momentum of the system does not change during the collision.

[1]

Markscheme

From F=ΔpΔt, zero net force on the system implies that Δp=0

OR

From Newton’s third law, the impulse delivered to A is equal but opposite to the impulse delivered to B, hence Δp=0 for the system  ✓
