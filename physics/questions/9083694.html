A balloon rises at a steady vertical velocity of 10 m s-1. An object is dropped from the balloon at a height of 40 m above the ground. Air resistance is negligible. What is the time taken for the object to hit the ground?

A.  10 s

B.  5 s

C.  4 s

D.  2 s

[1]

Markscheme

C

Examiners report

Even though over half the candidates are choosing the correct response it has a low discrimination index. Many are choosing D indicating that they forgot to take the velocity upward as negative.
