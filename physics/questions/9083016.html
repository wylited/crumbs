Show that the total kinetic energy Ek of the sphere when it rolls, without slipping, at speed v is  E K = 7 10 m v 2 .

 

[2]

Markscheme

Ek = Ek linear + Ek rotational

OR

E k = 1 2 m v 2 + 1 2 I ω 2   ✔

= 1 2 m v 2 + 1 2 × 2 5 m r 2 × ( v r ) 2  ✔

« = 7 10 m v 2 »
